My Story

A path through my own pain

From the age of 19 I lived with a bad back — I know first-hand what it means to search for answers for years. I tried almost everything, from conventional medicine to alternative practices.

But over time I realised: the body knows itself better than any doctor or pill — if you learn to listen.

I began my training through touch. Gradually, I restored my connection with my own body. When I added anatomy and muscle testing — the pieces fell into place. Step by step, my condition improved dramatically.

The positive feedback from early clients motivated me to keep sharing my experience, knowledge and hands. That's what I continue doing today — with the same energy as at the start.
