Important

Contraindications

We don't work when

  • Elevated body temperature
  • Acute inflammation or joint flare-ups
  • Serious systemic conditions
  • Infectious diseases in acute phase
  • First and last month of pregnancy

With caution

  • Menstruation — please let me know in advance
  • Pregnancy — only with doctor's approval
  • Pain medication or other drugs — inform before the session

Massage does not replace medical diagnosis or treatment.
